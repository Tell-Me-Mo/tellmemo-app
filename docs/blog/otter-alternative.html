Why Look for an Otter.ai Alternative?

Otter.ai is a solid product, but there are valid reasons to consider alternatives:

  • Privacy concerns — Otter stores all your meeting data on their servers
  • Cost at scale — $20/user/month adds up fast for larger teams
  • Limited search — Basic keyword search, no semantic/AI search across meetings
  • No self-hosting — Can't run it on your own infrastructure
  • Missing features — No real-time Q&A, limited RAG capabilities

Detailed Comparison: Top Otter.ai Alternatives

1. TellMeMo — Best Open Source Alternative

Best for: Teams who want complete control over their data, privacy-conscious organizations, and developers who want to customize their meeting intelligence.

TellMeMo is the only true open source alternative to Otter.ai. It's fully self-hostable and includes features Otter doesn't have.

What TellMeMo Does Better Than Otter

  • Self-hosting — Run on your own servers, your data never leaves
  • Real-time Q&A — AI answers questions during meetings, not just after
  • RAG search — Semantic search across all meetings and documents
  • Action tracking — Auto-extracts tasks with owner and deadline
  • Cost — Free software, only pay for AI API usage (~$0.01/meeting)

Pricing

TellMeMo is 100% free. You pay only for AI APIs:

  • Basic usage: ~$5-10/month for a small team
  • Heavy usage: ~$30-50/month for larger teams

2. Fireflies.ai — Best for Sales Teams

Fireflies is a commercial alternative focused on sales and revenue teams, with strong CRM integrations.

What Fireflies Does Better Than Otter

  • CRM integration — Auto-syncs meeting notes to Salesforce, HubSpot
  • Conversation intelligence — Tracks talk time, questions asked, sentiment
  • AskFred AI — ChatGPT-style interface for meeting search

Pricing

  • Free: Limited transcription credits
  • Pro: $19/user/month
  • Business: $39/user/month

Limitations

  • No self-hosting option
  • Data stored on their servers
  • Gets expensive for large teams

3. Fathom — Best Free Option for Zoom

Fathom offers unlimited free transcription for Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams.

What Fathom Does Better Than Otter

  • Truly free — Unlimited recordings on free plan
  • Speed — Instant summaries, no waiting
  • Simplicity — Minimal UI, easy to use

Pricing

  • Free: Unlimited recordings and summaries
  • Premium: $24/user/month for team features

Limitations

  • Limited search capabilities
  • No self-hosting
  • Fewer integrations than competitors

4. Jamie — Best for No-Bot Meetings

Jamie works without joining your meeting as a bot. It captures audio from your device directly.

What Jamie Does Better Than Otter

  • No bot — Doesn't join meetings, no "AI assistant joined" notification
  • Works anywhere — In-person meetings, phone calls, any audio
  • Privacy — Audio processed locally on your device

Pricing

  • Free: 5 meetings/month
  • Pro: $24/month unlimited

Limitations

  • Single-user focused
  • Limited team collaboration features
  • No self-hosting

5. Fellow — Best for Enterprise Compliance

Fellow is built for enterprise teams with strong governance, compliance, and integration needs.

What Fellow Does Better Than Otter

  • Compliance — SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR certified
  • Bot-free option — Can record without a visible bot
  • Meeting governance — Templates, agendas, feedback collection

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Pro: $9/user/month
  • Business: Custom pricing

Limitations

  • AI features require higher tiers
  • No self-hosting
  • Complex setup for full feature set
